My digital Education Story

Hello Learners,

This blog has to do with online learning via Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCS). Nowadays, lifelong learning is the best way to stay up to date with technology. Self-learning has special importance in terms of acquiring the necessary skills in our contemporary work environment. Some years ago I recognized that there was a potential in MOOCS and non-traditional education, especially if somebody tries to enrich a Curriculum vitae. With high rated specific online courses it is possible to obtain skills equivalent to a Bachelor’s degree or a master’s degree. In addition to MOOC certificates and how somebody can upload them in Linked in and other social media and platforms there is one big fact and the most important: The practical skills that employers love. In my case, my "nukes" in various interviews where the online courses in combination with work experience. A university degree is a proof of academic progress while continuous learning is an indication of personal and professional growth. Generally speaking it is not a good practice to underestimate traditional learning or omit to make an effort to learn something after the degree.
